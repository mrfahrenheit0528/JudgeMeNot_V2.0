How This Ledger Works

1
Score Recorded

When a judge or tabulator submits a score, the data is captured as a JSON snapshot.

2
Hash Generated

The snapshot, timestamp, and previous block's hash are combined and hashed with SHA-256.

3
Chain Linked

The new block is chained to the previous one. Any tampering breaks the entire chain.
